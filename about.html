Our Mission

At Adanehi Resources, we believe that every child and teenager deserves access to the tools, skills, and mentorship needed to navigate a digital-first world.

Based in Gonin Gora, Kaduna, our training centre offers hands-on, highly collaborative technology pathways. We focus on transforming passive consumers of technology into active creators, innovators, and problem-solvers.
